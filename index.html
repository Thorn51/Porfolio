About Me

I am a full-stack web developer, lifelong student, and creative problem solver. I am committed to designing and developing excellent user experiences.

When I'm not helping my team build amazing projects I love to spend time outdoors. I love cycling, taking long walks with my dog, and exploring the Rocky Mountains. My time in nature is a crtical part of my work-life balance and ensures that I'm performing at my peak for my team.

The Foundation

Building a solid foundation is the start of any great project.For me this starts with HTML. My focus during markup is to build a structure that is semantic and accessible. When these goals are met the site will provide access to search engine web crawlers, browser translation tools, and assistive technologies like screen readers.

The Art

When I'm satisfied that I've created a good foundation I'll turn to CSS. Whether working from a design, or creating from scratch, I'm committed to visually appealing content. My first goal with design it to make sure the content is accessible to any device the user chooses to use.

The Magic

Static content is no fun. I love content that is interactive and engaging. Using JavasScript, frameworks like React, and libraries like jQuery I'll code content so that springs to life. At the same time, I'll keep my focus on making sure the content is fast and lag free.
