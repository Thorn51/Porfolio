About

I am a full-stack web developer, lifelong student, and creative problem solver. I am committed to designing and developing excellent user experiences.

When I'm not helping my team build amazing projects I love to spend time outdoors cycling, taking long walks with my dog, and exploring the Rocky Mountains.

The Foundation

Building a solid foundation is the start of any great project. My focus during markup is to build a structure that is semantic and accessible. When these goals are met the site will provide access to search engine web crawlers, browser translation tools, and assistive technologies like screen readers.

The Art

When I'm satisfied that I've created a good foundation I'll turn to CSS. Whether working from a design, or creating from scratch, I'm committed to visually appealing content. My first goal with design it to make sure the content is accessible to any device the user chooses to use.

The Magic

Static content is no fun. I love content that is interactive and engaging. Using JavasScript, frameworks like React, and libraries like jQuery I'll focus on creating content that springs to life. While interactive content is fun it cannot come at the cost of performance and user experience. I'll always keep focused on fast load times and lag free content.
